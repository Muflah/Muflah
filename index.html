My Journey

Switching to product management has been one of the biggest decisions of my life that made very little sense when I started. Soon after starting my career as an engineer I realized something else that I was able to do efforlessly and it was delivering products. Coordinationg with cross functional teams and stakeholders to get things work was something that I was naturally good at and so it begun.

Opposed to engineering, that I studied and got license to work as, project management is something that I learned on the go. I'm one of those who learned the craft by doing with very little knowledge of how things work.
