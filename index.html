My Journey

Switching to product management has been one of the biggest decisions of my life—one that made very little sense when I started. Soon after beginning my career as an engineer, I realized there was something else I could do effortlessly: delivering products. Coordinating with cross-functional teams and stakeholders to get things working was something I was naturally good at—and so it began.

Unlike engineering, which I studied and earned a license to practice, project management is something I learned on the go. I'm one of those who learned the craft by doing, with very little initial knowledge of how things worked.

Product Management

It's been half a decade since I started working on products, and it’s now the major part of my career. So far, I’ve delivered and worked on a variety of products and enterprise platforms.

Agile Processes & Workflows
